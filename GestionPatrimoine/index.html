Asset Management

The problem

At a time of economic globalization, where the highly competitive business environment does not allow any mismanagement, most companies in developing countries, both public and private sectors, unfortunately face difficulties. enormous management. Like among others, the management of Asset is increasingly a concern of business leaders.

The proposed solution

the proposed solution This program will allow workers from within the company to request what they need to work within the company, whether it is always needed or for a short period, automatically, and modify and classify them and thus keep a record of each work. The official has the right to accept or reject orders according to the conditions, and he also has the right to add, delete or amend new workers and products. Ultimately, these two programs will facilitate interactions between workers and officials. The system should establish the organization and automate order management, in order to increase the reliability and efficiency of human effort and facilitate the tough tasks within the company.
