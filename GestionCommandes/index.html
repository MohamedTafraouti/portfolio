Order Management

The problem

At a time of economic globalization, where the highly competitive business environment does not allow any mismanagement, most companies in developing countries, both public and private sectors, unfortunately face difficulties. enormous management. Like among others, the management of Orders is increasingly a concern of business leaders.

The proposed solution

This software will allow you to automatically edit your quotes, modify them, classify them and thus keep a history of each prospect. Ultimately, it will be your major asset to get you paid on time. Depending on the payment terms granted to customers, you will be warned, by alerts, of any late payments. The recovery process is thus simplified: by e-mail, by simple letter or by registered mail. You can also get their contact details to call them directly. The System should put the organization and the automation of the management of the Orders, in order to increase the reliability, the efficiency of the human effort and to facilitate the arduous tasks within the company.
